Simple Omlette recepie



An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This clasic omlette combines beaten etts cooked to perfection,optionally field with your choice of chesse , vegetables, or meats.





    preperation time


  • total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • preperation : 5 minutes
  • cooking : 5 minutes


Ingredients


  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • Peper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or optionally
  • Optional fillings: chesse, diced vegetables, cooked meat, herbs.

    • Instructions

  • Beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and peper until they are well mixed. you can aff a tablespoon of water or milk for fluffier texture.

  • Heat the pan: place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil

  • Cook the omlete: once the butter is melted and bubblin, pour in eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surfance

  • Add fillings (optional): when the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your coshen fillings over one half of the omelette.

  • Fold and serve: as the omelette continues to cook carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filings, let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.

  • Enjoy: serve hot, with additional salt and peper if needed
